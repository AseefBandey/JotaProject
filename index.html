Biography

Diogo José Teixeira da Silva, widely known as Diogo Jota, was a Portuguese professional footballer and forward. He was born on December 4, 1996, in Massarelos, Portugal, and tragically passed away on July 3, 2025. Jota was renowned for his pace, finishing, and versatility. He began his career at Paços de Ferreira, later joining Atlético Madrid, and rose to fame with Wolverhampton Wanderers before moving to Liverpool in 2020. At Liverpool, he won the Premier League, FA Cup, and EFL Cups. Internationally, he represented Portugal, winning the UEFA Nations League in 2019 and 2025. Jota is remembered as a hardworking, talented, and respected figure in world football.
